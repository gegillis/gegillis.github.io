Who are we?

 

Christie and Chris's (C&C) Cooking Cabin is established in 2018. We are specialized in offering products and services related to amateur cooking activities. Whether you are a culinary novice or a gourmet guru, our professional chefs will share their hot tips and tricks that are sure to delight you, your friends, and your family. Each month we offer classes taught by area chefs from local restaurants, culinary schools, and catering companies. From sushi to salads to cake decorating, we have something for everyone! Our classes range from demonstration to full hands-on experience, and are always a delight to participate in.

 

Our history

  • 2018: C&C opened its first store around in West Lafayette, started offering cooking classes, and launchd our first website.
  • 2021: We hit another milestone with our 10th store in 2021 and would go on to add ten more in the next two years.
  • THE FUTURE: What does the future hold? Only one thing is certain—we can’t wait to make more delicious memories together. Make More. Gather Often.
